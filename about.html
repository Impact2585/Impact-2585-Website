About Us

  Since our rookie year in the FIRST Robotics Competition in 2008, Bellaire High School Robotics Team has provided students the opportunity to apply lessons learned in the classroom on a real world problem. With FIRST Robotics Competition and VEX Robotics Competition, our students can learn and be inspired to pursue a career centered around the science, technology, engineering or mathematics (STEM) fields. Throughout the years, the team has gone through ups and downs in performance. However, the focus of a student driven team remains as we allow our students with a hands-off approach to feel fully in control of what they build.
